About ICECE

The IEEE international Conference on Emerging Materials and Nano-Devices for Advanced Computing aims to explore the transformative role of novel materials and nanoscale devices in the evolution of advanced computing systems. As computational demands increase and traditional technologies reach their limits, the conference will investigate into essential research that leverages new materials and nano-engineering techniques to push the boundaries of processing power, efficiency, and miniaturization.

The conference will cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to, the development of two-dimensional materials like graphene, transition metal dichalcogenides (TMDs), and organic semiconductors for next-generation transistors. Special attention will be given to the role of quantum dots, spintronic devices, and topological insulators in realizing high-performance, low-power computing systems.Researchers will also present advancements in the fabrication and integration of nanoscale devices, such as memristors, quantum computers, and neuromorphic circuits, which are poised to revolutionize data processing capabilities and computational architectures.
