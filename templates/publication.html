Indexing and Databases

All accepted papers presented at the ICASPACE 2025 Conference will be indexed in leading databases, including Scopus, IEEE Xplore, Web of Science, and Google Scholar. This ensures that your research gains global visibility and contributes to the academic and professional advancement in the field of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE).

Guidelines for Authors

Authors submitting papers to the ICASPACE 2025 Conference in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE) must adhere to the following guidelines: Original Contributions: Papers must be original and not previously published. Experimental & Simulation Studies: Submissions should include experimental investigations, simulation studies with practical results, or case studies of industrial significance. Formatting: Authors should follow the provided paper formatting templates available on the conference website. Plagiarism Check:All submissions will undergo a plagiarism check to ensure originality. Paper Length: Papers should be within the specified word limit (usually 4 to 6 pages).

Paper Submission Guidelines

Every article submitted to ICASPACE 2025 must meet the following criteria:

  • Consisting of experimental investigation with specific conclusions
  • Original Contribution, not published earlier
  • Case studies of industrial importance
  • Simulation studies with practical demonstration/results
  • Design of Instruments / Systems / Software

Conference Proceedings with Springer

It is planned to publish the peer-reviewed and selected papers of the conference as proceedings with Springer in their prestigious “Lecture Notes in Electrical Engineering” series. For detailed instructions for authors and editors of conference proceedings, kindly visit the Conference Proceedings Link. Select papers from the conference will be published by Springer as a proceedings book volume. Springer will conduct quality checks on the accepted papers, and only papers that pass these checks will be published.

Note: Springer Nature does not charge any money for publication of Non-Open Access content. Abstracts/extended abstracts and short papers (less than 4 pages) are not considered for publication.
